Algorithm for FCFS Scheduling (Batch 1)

1. Define a structure for processes with AT, BT, CT, TAT, and WT.
2. Read the number of processes and their Arrival Time (AT) and Burst Time (BT).
3. Sort the processes based on their Arrival Time.
4. Initialize current_time = 0.
5. For each process:
   a. If current_time < AT, set current_time = AT (CPU is idle).
   b. current_time = current_time + BT.
   c. CT = current_time.
   d. TAT = CT - AT.
   e. WT = TAT - BT.
6. Calculate average WT and TAT.
7. Print the Gantt chart metrics for each process.
