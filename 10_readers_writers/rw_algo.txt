Algorithm for Experiment 10: Readers-Writers with Writer Priority

1. Initialize semaphores:
   a. resource_access: Binary semaphore for resource access.
   b. read_count_access: Binary semaphore for updating read_count.
   c. write_count_access: Binary semaphore for updating write_count.
   d. service_queue: Semaphore to ensure writers have priority over readers.
2. Reader Logic:
   a. Wait on 'service_queue'.
   b. Wait on 'read_count_access' and increment 'read_count'.
   c. If 'read_count' is 1, wait on 'resource_access' (lock the book).
   d. Post 'read_count_access' and 'service_queue'.
   e. Perform reading.
   f. Wait on 'read_count_access' and decrement 'read_count'.
   g. If 'read_count' is 0, post 'resource_access' (unlock the book).
   h. Post 'read_count_access'.
3. Writer Logic:
   a. Wait on 'write_count_access' and increment 'write_count'.
   b. If 'write_count' is 1, wait on 'service_queue' (block new readers).
   c. Post 'write_count_access'.
   d. Wait on 'resource_access' and perform writing.
   e. Post 'resource_access'.
   f. Wait on 'write_count_access' and decrement 'write_count'.
   g. If 'write_count' is 0, post 'service_queue' (unblock readers).
   h. Post 'write_count_access'.
4. Start multiple reader and writer threads to demonstrate the priority and sync.
